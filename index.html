Pens and Paper

The aim of our Pens and Paper sessions is for children to stretch their imaginative writing skills and grow their love for literature and language. In a small-group setting, they will:


learn and experiment with a variety of poetic and short prose forms
practice elements of the writing process such as brainstorming, planning, drafting, and polishing
expand their vocabulary
explore their feelings and develop a greater capacity for self-expression
At the end of term, they will leave with a portfolio of written work that captures their childhood ingenuity!


About Me

I am a qualified teacher, a parent, and an enthusiastic reader and writer. I have a PGCE in English from UCL and a collection of degrees in the humanities, including a Ph.D. in Literature and a Foundation certificate in Counselling and Psychotherapy. I believe that when children are encouraged to develop their natural interest in wordplay, poems, and stories, they become more ambitious thinkers and thoughtful humans.
